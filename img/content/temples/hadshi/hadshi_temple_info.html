Hadshi Temple

Location: Near tikona Vadgoan Maval, Pune,Maharashtra

Mountain range: Western Ghats

 

Description:

Hadshi Temple, also known as Sri Satya Sai Pandurang Kshetra, Pune is a temple around 45 Kms from Pune in a beautiful scenic village Hadshi. The temple is dedicated to Lord Vitthal Rukmani.

Hadshi Temple is spread in a vast area of around 300 acres and surrounded by mesmerizing views of mountains all around. The temple is very clean and very well managed by Sri Satya Sai Trust. There are two huge gardens on both sides of the temple to sit, relax and spend some time with nature. The view from these gardens is amazing as the whole temple is surrounded by picturesque views.

There is a huge museum here. The museum is not of art, scientific or any historic things. But the Museum has a lot of Idols depicting the saints and culture of Maharashtra.

Hadshi Temple also has an Adventure Sports section called a Valley of Adventure They have Sky Cycle, Zip Line, Archery, Gun Shooting, Rock Climbing, Gyro, Bull Ride, Melt Down, Bungy and ATV.

Hadshi Temple is no doubt one of the best one-day picnic spots around Pune with Kids, especially in Monsoon when the climate is beautiful around with lots of greenery and mesmerizing views around.

How to Reach:

From Pune:There are two routes to reach Hadshi from Pune.

  1. Route I: Pune – Bavdhan – Pirangut – Paud – Chhale – Hadshi
  2. Route II: Pune – Sus – Nande – Chhale – Hadshi

Notes:

  • Carry your own food
  • Umbrellas are of no use, Carry raincoats and windcheater during monsoon
  • Charges:
    1. Parking Charge: 20 Rs
    2. Museum (Optional): 150 Rs
    3. Valley for Adventure (Optional): 350 Rs
  • Timings:
    • Morning - 4:00 am - 12:00 pm
    Evening - 4:00 pm - 8:00 pm
