Independence Day Special Group Tour To Leh-Ladakh

Lets explore the Leh Ladakh with Hills and Highways

❮ ❯

Leh Ladakh Group tour COST: Rs. 16666/- Per Person 

About:

Being one of the most favoured destinations in India, Leh-Ladakh is on everyone’s bucket list. And to help you fulfil your dream holiday. If you are leisure seeker, nature lover or history enthusiasts then this is the perfect package for you.



Rendezvous:

Day – 01 (11th Aug 2020): Arrival at Leh- Arrival at Leh airport, to be received by our representative and transfered to the Hotel. Check in to the hotel for a Complete day of rest where in welcome tea and coffee will be served by the Hotel. After rest visit  Overnight stay at Leh hotel.

Leh Palace and Shanti Stupa.

Day – 02 (12th Aug 2020): Leh city tour – After breakfast drive for full day sight seeing to the…..

Likir, Alchi Monastery, Gurudwara Shri Pathar Sahib, Sangam  (Confluence of Zanskar & Indus River)& Hall Of Fame & Flag Retrieval Ceremony Back to Leh by evening. Overnight stay at Leh hotel.

Day – 03 (13th Aug 2020): Leh to Nubra – After Breakfast check out from the hotel and drive to Nubra Valley via Khardungla (Highest Motorable road in the World,18,380 ft). Arrive at Diskit by Afternoon. Rest of the day is free to explore Diskit, HunderVillages and camel Safari in Sand Dunes between Diskit  and Hunder Village.  Overnight stay at Nubra Valley.(Hotel/Camp)

ATV RIDE & OTHER ADVENTURE ACTIVITIES (own Cost)

CULTURAL PROGRAMME

Day – 04 (14th Aug 2020): Nubra to Turtuk :- After Breakfast check out from the hotel and drive to Turtuk, Turtuk is the last outpost in India after which the Pakistan-controlled Gilgit-Baltistan begins. Turtuk is one of the gateways to the Siachen Glacier.

Thang (Last Village Of India) Overnight stay at Nubra Valley.(Hotel/Camp)

Day 05 (15th Aug 2020): Nubra to Pangong – After Breakfast, visit Diskit Monastery and drive to Pangong Lake (14,500 ft), Arrive Pangong, it is the highest salt water Lake in the World, shared by two countries India & China. Enjoy the beauty of the lake on the Banks of pangong while appreciating the changing Colors and fascinating high altitude of the Lake. Overnight stay at Pangong Lake hotel/camp.

Day 06 (16th Aug 2020):  Pangong Lake to Leh –  After Breakfast leave for Leh through Changla Pass17,350 ft., it is the third highest motorable road in the world. 1200 hrs. . Packed or  Drive back to Leh by the same route. Overnight stay at Hotel.

Hemis Monastery/Thiksey Monastery/Shey Place/Three Idoits School (Druk White Lotus School) 

Day 07 (17th Aug 2020): Drop at Airport – Transfer to airport and fly back to Delhi /Mumbai for your onward journey with memories of A LifetimeTrip.
