Best Places To Experience Paragliding In India

Paragliding is one such adventure sport that lets you experience the thrill of soaring high in the sky without a prior flying experience. It also gives you a wonderful opportunity to enjoy the beauty of the region below you. And with so many paragliding spots in India, you can easily experience the joy of this aerial sport without further ado.

Here are 9 paragliding places in India that offer a memorable experience:

  1. Paragliding in Kamshet, Maharashtra

Kamshet is the favorite hotspot of adventure junkies who meet up at this place regularly to practice, learn this adventure sport or just have some fun ride in the air. Consider paragliding in Kamshet as you will get to soar over mountains, meadows, water bodies like the Indrayani River and the Pavana Lake.

  1. Paragliding in Solang Valley and Bir Billing, Himachal Pradesh

It’s not without a reason that Himachal Pradesh is called as the “Paragliding Capital of India”. Paragliding in Solang Valley and Bir Billing is an experience worth remembering. Become one with the surrounding as you glide through the high sky, pass through green tea plantations, endless stretches of meadows and view the snow-capped mountains.

  1. Paragliding in Nainital, Uttarakhand

Paragliding in Uttarakhand offers unlimited fun and excitement. Nainital, nestled amidst the beautiful mountains is a popular destination where you can do tandem paragliding. You can choose from – Medium (3 minutes), High flying (15 minutes) and Long Flying (30 minutes) sessions. Paragliding in Mussoorie is incomparable as witnessing the grand panoramic view of this small town from a height of 10,000 feet is sheer bliss!

  1. Paragliding in Shillong, Meghalaya

Shillong in Meghalaya is the perfect destination if you want to witness some heart-thumping joyride. The blue skies, snow-clad mountains, tall trees, and wide stretches of landscape expanses will make your ride unforgettable.

  1. Paragliding in Gangtok, Sikkim

Soak in the natural grandeur of Gangtok with either a medium range or high range paragliding session. The medium range session is of about 10 minutes while the high rage session lasts for 20 minutes. The aerial views of the landscapes below are simply breathtaking.

  1. Paragliding in Nandi Hills, Karnataka

Nandi Hills, which is a 60 km drive from Bangalore, is a popular paragliding destination in Karnataka. Paragliding here takes place at more than 4500 feet. The pleasant climate and plateaued terrain makes it an ideal location to learn and practice paragliding.

  1. Paragliding in Vagamon, Kerala

A beautiful hill station in Kerala, Vagamon offers a delightful paragliding experience to the tourists. The adventure, which lasts for good 20 minutes, will give you a magnificent view of waterfalls, lush forests and tea plantations.

  1. Paragliding in Yelagiri, Tamil Nadu

Yelagiri in Tamil Nadu is an ideal paragliding destination for those going for their first paragliding experience. Paragliding from a height of 2200 meters offers an unparalleled view of the scenic hills around.

  1. Paragliding in Arambol, Goa

Paragliding in Arambol Beach makes for a truly enchanting experience. Cruising through the gushes of air, watching the beach and the sea will give you an adrenaline surge.
